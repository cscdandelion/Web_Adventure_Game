The Prince Adventure

Once upon a time, there was a princess Elizabeth who lived in a mysterious castle with lots of treasure.

She is known as the most beautiful princess of the dynasty, and one day, the princess fell in love with a prince

Unfortunately, Orca, who also loves Elizabeth, eagered to possess her, so he provoked a war and destroyed half

of the castle and made it into Dungeon. Orca imprisoned Elizabeth and threatened the prince to leave her,

otherwise he would kill her. At last, the prince left sadly.

Now, you are the prince, and you get back with your army and have a chance to rescue Elizabeth.
